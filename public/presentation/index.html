Jupiter Tripwire

A Solana narrative radar that converts Jupiter token, price, and swap route signals into explainable builder tripwires.

What It Uses

  • Tokens V2 for metadata, audit fields, liquidity, and 5m/1h/24h stats.
  • Price V3 for compact USD price snapshots across a watchlist.
  • Swap V2 order as a quote-only route-health probe without signing trades.

Why It Matters

Builders need to know whether a Solana narrative is moving now and whether users can still route into it cleanly. Tripwire joins market acceleration with Jupiter route health and turns the result into concrete product ideas.
